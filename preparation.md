### What to bring with you
We're glad you're joining us for this workshop! Here is an overview of what we suggest bringing and items to prepare ahead of time.

1. Your laptop with Praat installed.
2. An external mouse (whatever works with your laptop). We will try to provide extras, but please bring one with you if you are able.
3. We will be using USBs for the data so if your laptop doesn't have a USB port, please bring an adapter.
